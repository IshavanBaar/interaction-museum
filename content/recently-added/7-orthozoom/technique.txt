Title: OrthoZoom Scroller

----

Description: To perform panning and zooming in a 1D space, the OrthoZoom Scroller requires only a mouse. It improves target acquisition in very large one-dimensional spaces. Panning is performed along the slider dimension while zooming is performed along the orthogonal one.
